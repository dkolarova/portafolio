Hi There!

Currently based in Prague, Czech Republic

Hello! My name is Diana Kolarova, and I'm a passionate UI/UX designer. I was born in the Czech Republic but grew up in Latin America, where I spent 11 years of my life. My multicultural background has shaped my perspective and creativity, allowing me to bring cultural understanding and innovation into my design work.

I'm a student with a background in UI/UX design currently studying at 42 Prague and an IT school since May 2025. During my time at 42, I became a Tutor, where I volunteer to help fellow students with their learning journey.

I’m currently at the beginning of my programming journey, learning C and C++, and working toward specializing in mobile app development. My goal is to combine my design background with strong technical skills to build intuitive and impactful mobile applications.

My Path Into Tech

My journey into tech began through UI/UX design, where I developed a strong interest in creating intuitive and meaningful user experiences. Over time, I became increasingly curious about the deeper side of how products are built—especially in mobile app development. To explore this, I started with online courses and later joined 42 Prague in May 2025 to gain a more structured and in-depth understanding. At 42, I’ve been learning low-level programming in C and C++, along with core computer science concepts such as operating systems, memory management, and working in Unix environments. Along the way, I’ve also supported other students by sharing knowledge and helping them navigate challenges, which has strengthened both my technical skills and my ability to explain complex ideas clearly.

I love understanding user needs and behavior, which helps me design digital experiences that are intuitive, beautiful, and meaningful.
